## üß† 200 IQ META PLAYS

### 1. The "Dead Man Walking" Play

**Situation:** You're 10+ behind surge, zone is far, no mats.

**Play:**
1. Intentionally rotate THROUGH storm (not around)
2. Hit every barrel/campfire on the path
3. Teams assume you're dead and stop watching
4. Pop out of storm INSIDE zone with element of surprise
5. Free tags on teams focused elsewhere

**Why it works:** Nobody expects storm rotates. They're looking at zone edge.

### 2. The "Surge Sacrifice"

**Situation:** Partner is knocked, you're below surge, zone moving.

**Play:**
1. DON'T revive immediately
2. Use the 30 seconds of knockout timer
3. Get surge tags while they're down
4. Revive right before they die
5. Both rotate together with surge solved

**Risk:** High. If you can't get tags, partner bleeds out. Only use when desperate.

### 3. The "Launch Pad Bait"

**Situation:** You have launch pad, team watching your rotate.

**Play:**
1. Build up like you're going to pad
2. Place pad but DON'T use it
3. Drop down and rotate ground
4. Enemy expects you in air, looking wrong direction
5. Or: Let them pad to YOUR pad location while you're already gone

### 4. The "Mat Poverty Rotate"

**Situation:** Sub-100 total mats, need to cross to zone.

**Play:**
1. Identify LOWEST contest path (not shortest)
2. Only build when actively shot at
3. Use natural cover (rocks, trees, buildings)
4. Sprint through open ground - building 1 wall wastes more time than just running
5. Let storm push you - teams can't spray through storm

**Pro tip:** Below 50 mats? Just don't build. You can't win a fight anyway, so save them for one emergency wall.

### 5. The "Zone Edge Camp" (For 35-45ms Ping)

**Situation:** You have high ping, can't piece control fight.

**Play:**
1. Rotate to zone edge EARLY (before half-half)
2. Dig into natural terrain or existing builds
3. Let teams rotate TO you, not past you
4. AR beam teams who pass your position
5. NEVER chase - let them come

**Why this works for high ping:**
- AR fights neutralize ping difference (bullet travel time)
- You see THEM before they see you
- First shot advantage > edit plays
- You choose when to engage

### Weapons Priority (35-45ms)

| Rank | Weapon | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Sniper (any) | No ping involved in bullet travel |
| 2 | Gold/Purple AR | First shot accuracy, long range beam |
| 3 | SMG | Spray through builds, don't need to piece control |
| 4 | Shotgun (pump > tac) | One shot = one chance, no need for edits |
| AVOID | Pistol/Semi-auto | Requires fast successive inputs = ping disadvantage |

### Building Adjustments

**NEVER DO (on high ping):**
- 50/50 piece control fights
- Edit plays on enemy boxes
- Retake during active pressure
- Tunnel fights (too many edits)

**ALWAYS DO:**
- Prefire every shot (bullets leave late)
- Let enemy edit first, THEN react
- Use cone floors > wall ramps (less inputs)
- Highground retake ONLY when they're not looking
- AR from distance before closing gap

### The "Ping Proof" Playstyle

1. **Early Game:** Land uncontested. Never fight off-spawn.
2. **Mid Game:** Rotate FIRST. Be in zone before anyone else.
3. **Late Game:** Take layer EARLY. Don't fight for it last second.
4. **Endgame:** Hold position. Let teams come to you. Never push.

### Deadside Creation

**Definition:** Deadside = side of zone with no/few teams

**How to identify deadside:**
1. Watch killfeed during storm close
2. Count gunshots by direction
3. Note where teams rotate FROM (that side = emptied)
4. Rotate TO the side teams just left

**Why it matters:**
- Less RNG in late game
- Guaranteed safe side for tarps
- Free refreshes on teams who don't know deadside
